About Us


Welcome to Paul AN Co., a premier name in the granite industry. Established by Mr. Laju Peter and Mr. Kiran GR, we have been leading the way for over a decade. Our shared passion and unique skill sets make us a powerful team. Mr. Laju Peter, with 20 years of technical expertise, and Mr. Kiran GR, with 10 years of experience in management, documentation, and operations, ensure that we deliver the highest quality products and services to our clients.





Our Facilities

We operate a state-of-the-art production unit and warehouse spread over 300,000 square feet, strategically located 90 kilometers from Bangalore and 140 kilometers from Chennai. Our corporate office is based in Bangalore, and all our exports are handled through the Chennai port, ensuring efficient and timely delivery worldwide.
